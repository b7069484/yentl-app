## Strategic Thesis

The best Yentl is not a general-purpose fact-check chatbot. It is a live, source-anchored media-literacy companion that preserves context while helping users inspect claims, evidence, rhetoric, and uncertainty.

Every committee lane converges on the same truth: the product concept is strong, but the launch story is ahead of runtime protections and evaluation evidence.

## Ownership Model

The biggest risk is everyone improving their slice while product truth remains inconsistent. Create four cross-cutting owners:

- Trust owner: privacy, consent, security, compliance, source policy.
- Evidence owner: prompts, fact-checking, corpora, human review.
- Experience owner: UI, UX, mobile, extension, accessibility.
- Launch owner: pricing, GTM, comms, support, analytics.

Each feature must pass all four owners before launch.

## Final Strategic Call

Yentl has earned continued investment. It has not earned open launch. The highest-leverage move is a convergence sprint, then a private beta with strong language: `research preview`, `source-backed`, `not authoritative`, `designed to help you inspect evidence`.

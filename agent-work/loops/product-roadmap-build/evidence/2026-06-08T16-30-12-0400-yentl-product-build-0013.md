## Slice

Verification prompts now receive ownership-aware claim context, so provisional and confirmed fact-checking can distinguish a proposition's truth from whether the visible speaker asserted, quoted, reported, denied, mocked, questioned, or uncertainly owned that proposition.

This continues the speaker-attribution / conversation-intelligence work past extraction, synthesis, and Devil's Advocate into the actual verification layer.

- Added prompt helpers that render `SOURCE_CONTEXT` and `CLAIM_CONTEXT` as disambiguating context, explicitly not evidence.
